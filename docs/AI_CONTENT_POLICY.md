# AI Content Policy

## Disclosure Requirements

| Content Type | Badge Required |
|---|---|
| Human-written, no AI | None |
| Human-written, AI suggested edits | None |
| AI-generated summary (for low-data mode) | "AI-generated summary" |
| Human + AI co-wrote | "AI-assisted" badge |
| Mostly/entirely AI | "AI-generated" badge |

## Violations

Claiming human authorship for AI-generated content is a policy violation.

**Consequences:**
1. First offense: Warning, content labeled
2. Second offense: Strike, 30-day publishing suspension
3. Third offense: Account termination

## Premium Human Review

Authors can pay for:
- Human editors (pre-publication)
- Professional fact-checkers
- "Verified Accuracy" badge

This allows opt-out from AI moderation assistance.
