# Ombudsman

## Purpose

The Ombudsman independently reviews moderation decisions and publishes findings. They are NOT employed by or controlled by seethru.media leadership.

## Selection

Selected by a 3-person committee:
1. Representative from journalism organization (SPJ, Reporters Committee, etc.)
2. Representative from civil liberties organization (EFF, ACLU, etc.)
3. Representative elected by creator community

## Term

- 3 years, renewable once
- Cannot be removed by founder
- Removal only by 2/3 Committee vote for cause

## Responsibilities

- Review random sample of moderation decisions monthly
- Publish public findings
- Recommend policy changes
- Investigate user complaints about moderation

## Independence

- Findings published publicly; founder cannot suppress
- Budget set by Committee
- Reports to Committee, not to seethru.media leadership
