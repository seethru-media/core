# Advertising Policy

## Political Ads

**BANNED.** No political advertising of any kind:
- Candidate ads
- Issue advocacy
- PAC/SuperPAC content

## Allowed Advertising

- Product/service ads (non-political)
- Non-political non-profit promotion (with disclosure)

## Revenue Priority

We minimize advertising. Revenue priority:

1. Donations
2. Subscriptions
3. Creator revenue share
4. Ethical ads (last resort)

## Advertiser Rules

- No veto over content placement
- No topic exclusions
- Public advertiser list (quarterly)
