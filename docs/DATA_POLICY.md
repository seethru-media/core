# Data Policy

## What We Collect

| Data | Why | Stored Where | Opt-Out |
|---|---|---|---|
| Page views (aggregated) | Analytics | Cloudflare | N/A (anonymous) |
| Reading time | Quality signals | Server-side | Yes |
| Search queries | Improve search | Server-side | Yes |
| Feed preferences | Personalization | Server-side | Yes |
| Geographic area (coarse) | Local content | Server-side | Yes |

## What We Don't Do

- ❌ Cookies
- ❌ LocalStorage for tracking
- ❌ Fingerprinting
- ❌ Third-party trackers
- ❌ Sell data

## Your Rights

- **View**: See all data we have about you
- **Export**: One-click download
- **Delete**: Permanent deletion (30-day grace period)
- **Opt-out**: Use with zero personalization

---

*No cookies. Server-side sessions only (opt-in).*
