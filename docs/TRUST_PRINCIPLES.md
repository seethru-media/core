# seethru.media Trust Principles

## Our Commitments to Readers

### 1. You Own Your Attention
- No engagement optimization
- No outrage-maximizing algorithms
- Geographic daily brief first, rabbit holes second

### 2. You Know Who Made This
- Every piece of content shows author, source, and date
- AI-generated content is always labeled
- No anonymous accusations of named individuals

### 3. We Don't Read Your Messages
- DMs are end-to-end encrypted
- We cannot access message content
- We can only act on user reports

### 4. We Don't Track Without Disclosure
- No cookies
- All tracked data visible to you
- Full opt-out available
- We never sell data

### 5. Our Finances Are Public
- See our costs and revenue in real-time
- Know who funds us
- Understand where money goes

### 6. Governance is Forkable
- All rules are public and versioned
- Anyone can propose changes
- If you don't like our governance, fork it

---

*These principles are enforced by the Platform Charter and public accountability.*
